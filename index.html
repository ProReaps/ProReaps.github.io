About me

Again, I'm Angel Sandoval, born in 2002, a student from the University of Advanced Technologies based in Zapopan, Jalisco. An aspiring game designer and developer.

I started programming in 2017 at the ripe age of 15 years old, when I needed to pick from a very small pool of majors back in highschool. I just chose the one that seemed the most interesting. Being a bit of a gamer, I figured I'd be able to make games if I got good enough. Since then I've kept doing it in both personal and school projects.
I specialize in working with game engines and mechanics. I've been designing and coding games since 2022.

My current goal is to grow in the game development and design space. Working on a variety of projects so as to learn the most skills possible and be as flexible as is required nowadays.

My main hobbies at the moment align quite a bit with the space, I play a few games both with friends and alone, and partake in a dnd campaign where players carefully concoct their character's backstory (with the dm) and have it be a part of the overarching narrative.
